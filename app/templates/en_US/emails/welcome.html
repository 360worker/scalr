How to get started
Every Scalr user is different. Which of these categories do you belong to? Please mention these when you contact us and make sure to ask yourself the question. You will save a lot of time.

Padawan
You have a very short or no experience as a sysadmin but you’re serious about moving to the cloud. You're running a limited number of servers on a classic stack (LAMP or similar). You want to make your life easier. Check our Youtube channel to see how Scalr works and the tutorial on a WordPress installation. You may also contact us for a 15-min demo.

Knight
You are serious about technology, you run your website really well -on multiple servers. Our experience is different but you spent long hours in 1-2-1 with Tux. You know what you are looking for -and it’s a complex project. After looking at our docs, you want to be contacted by a Scalr representative to discuss your project and start building your wet dream infrastructure.
